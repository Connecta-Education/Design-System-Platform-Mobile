glyph backdropSingle brand color, slightly darker than the block ground (~12–18% darker). Never a different hue — it should feel like the ground is dimensional, not collaged.
scale80–140% of the short edge for hero blocks; ~75% for compact accents. Always bleeds off at least one edge — the glyph reads as space, not a logo.
photoInset 14–16px on all sides, 12–14px radius. Photo content contained — never bleeds to the block edge. Leaves the bottom third as colored ground for wordmark + line.
wordmarkBelow the photo, left-aligned, 22–28px tall depending on block size. Color follows the same contrast rules as the photo-card system.
text-only blocksFor compact / social use, drop the photo. The glyph becomes a small accent in the upper-right (~75% short edge). Tagline below the wordmark.
don'tDon't outline the glyph. Don't gradient the glyph. Don't put the glyph over the photo. Don't mix two glyphs on one block. Don't fully contain the glyph — it must bleed.
