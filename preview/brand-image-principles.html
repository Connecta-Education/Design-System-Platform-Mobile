Do

Warm, natural light. Candid moments (a teen on a bus, a kid at a desk, hands on a notebook). Real classroom textures. Grain acceptable. Black-and-white OK for editorial pieces — never for product chrome.

Don't

Cool / teal grading. Posed family-on-couch stock. Heavy filters or blur. Faces front-and-center as the hero. Staged classroom scenes. Generic "education" stock photography.

Do — for children

Shoulders, hands, devices, environments. Backs-of-heads. Anonymity-respectful framing — kids look like themselves, never like models. EU schools, EU streets, EU light.

Don't

Identifiable close-ups of named children without consent docs in `assets/`. Kids holding the product like an ad. American suburb aesthetics. Forced diversity quotas — cast real, cast local.
